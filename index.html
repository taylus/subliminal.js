subliminal.js is a JavaScript library that gets into your <head>

  1. Include the library: <script src='subliminal.js'></script>

  2. Register an image: subliminal.registerImage(url, 1000, 5000, '0.1')
    This image will appear for one second, every 5 seconds, at 10% opacity.

  3. Take over the world: your subliminal messages will slowly impart your commands into the minds of your viewers.
